Ribs/Paigu 排骨

My number-one favorite dish growing up, Dad's Paigu was always a treat to find on the dinner table. He still refuses to give me the exact recipe, but here's a decent approximation.

Ingredient Approximate Amount
Ribs However Much You Got From The Store
Cooking Wine A Splash
Dark Soy Sauce 1/2 the amount of Light Soy Sauce
Light Soy Sauce 2x the amount of Dark Soy Sauce
Salt A Sprinkle
Sugar A Pinch
  1. Throw the ribs into a pan with oil, then add cooking wine.

  2. Cut up the ginger while the ribs are cooking

  3. Throw the ginger into the pan and fry until the ribs are brown on the outside

  4. Fry until the sides are golden brown
  5. Turn the heat down and cover the pan for 5-10 minutes to allow the ribs to cook further
  6. Add dark soy sauce, light soy sauce, salt, and sugar

  7. Cover the pan again and let simmer for 10-20 minutes or however long you want, until tender.
  8. Serve!
