Belt Fish/Diaoyu 带鱼

Dad is making this with Belt Fish, which are kind of ugly dudes, but I think you could probably re apply this recipe to like any kind of fish, to be honest. Genuinely, I think this is the flavor profile he uses for every food ever, but especially with fish-- so this is a one-size-fits-all recipe

Ingredients
Ingredient Approximate Amount
Belt Fish However Much You Got From The Store
Garlic 3 Cloves
Ginger A Little
Green Onion 2 stalks
Cooking Wine 2x the amount of Soy Sauce
Light Soy Sauce 1/2 of the amount of cooking wine
Salt A Sprinkle
Sugar A Sprinkle
  1. Wash the fish and cut into chunks, slicing slits into them
  2. Cut up garlic, ginger, green onion
  3. Cover a frying pan bottom with oil
  4. Fry until the sides are golden brown
  5. Cover the pan while the fish fries
  6. Put the ginger, garlic, and the bottom parts green onion into the pan with the fish
  7. Add cooking wine, soy sauce, salt, and sugar
  8. Cover and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes on medium.
  9. Add the green onion into the pan
  10. Cook for 2-3 more minutes
  11. Serve!
